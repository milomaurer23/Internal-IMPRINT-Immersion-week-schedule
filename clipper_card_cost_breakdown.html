Concept & Fundamentals
Transit Debit Cards vs. Flat Budgets
🎫 What is a Clipper Card?

Think of Clipper like a reloadable transit gift card. You load it with funds, hand it to a scholar, and they tap at the gate. Balances stay on the card until spent.

🔄 Why Not the $30 Budget from 2025?

In 2025 (IMPRINT), cards were used for exactly one travel day. A $30 load covered that single day easily. This year, we have multiple travel days across two cohorts, requiring a strategic shift in load values.
