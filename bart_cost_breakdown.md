## 💳 Clipper Card Guidelines & Fee Rules

* **System Access:** A Clipper card (or mobile version) is required for every traveler. Cash is not accepted at the fare gates.
* **Apple Pay & Google Pay (Highly Recommended):**
  * Travelers can add a digital Clipper card to Apple Wallet or Google Wallet for **free** ($0 activation fee).
  * This bypasses the queues at station kiosks and completely avoids the physical card fee.
* **Physical Clipper Card Fee:**
  * Kiosks charge a **$3.00 fee** to issue a physical plastic card.
* **Suggested Budget / Load Amount:**
  * Load **$15.00 per traveler** initially. This covers the JLABS round-trip with a small buffer. For the full multi-day program, see the main Clipper Card Plan.
